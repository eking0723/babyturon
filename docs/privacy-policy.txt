Privacy Policy

Media Platform for Digital Artists

Last Updated: 3/8/2026

1. Introduction
    This Privacy Policy explains how the Media Platform for Digital Artists (“the Platform”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, processes, and protects personal data of users.

    We are committed to protecting user privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including:
        Republic Act No. 10173 – Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Philippines)
        General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users located in the European Economic Area (EEA)

    By using the Platform, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Personal Data We Collect
    We may collect the following categories of personal data:
    2.1 Account Information
        Username
        Email address
        Password (stored in encrypted form)

    2.2 Profile Information
        Profile picture
        Artist bio
        Other information voluntarily provided by the user

    2.3 User Content
        Uploaded artworks
        Artwork titles and descriptions
        Comments and interactions (likes, comments)

    2.4 Usage Information
        Device information
        IP address
        Log data and platform activity
        Interaction data within the platform

3. Purpose of Data Processing
    We process personal data for the following purposes:
        To create and manage user accounts
        To enable uploading and displaying of artworks
        To allow users to interact through likes and comments
        To maintain user profiles
        To improve platform functionality and user experience
        To monitor and maintain system security

    Data is processed only for legitimate and specified purposes consistent with GDPR and the Data Privacy Act.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)
    Where GDPR applies, personal data is processed based on:
        User consent (e.g., account registration)
        Performance of a contract (providing platform services)
        Legitimate interests (platform security and service improvement)
        Legal obligations, where required

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
    We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

    Personal data may only be shared in the following circumstances:
        With service providers necessary for operating the platform (e.g., hosting services)
        When required by law or government authorities
        To protect the rights, security, or property of the platform or its users

    Any third-party service providers are required to comply with applicable data protection laws.

6. Data Retention
    Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to:
        Provide the platform services
        Comply with legal obligations
        Resolve disputes or enforce policies

    Users may request deletion of their data as described below.

7. User Rights
    In accordance with the GDPR and the Data Privacy Act of 2012, users have the following rights:
        Right to Access – Request access to personal data held about them
        Right to Rectification – Request correction of inaccurate data
        Right to Erasure – Request deletion of personal data
        Right to Object – Object to certain forms of data processing
        Right to Data Portability – Request a copy of their personal data
        Right to Withdraw Consent – Withdraw previously given consent at any time

    Requests may be submitted through the platform’s contact or support channels.

8. Data Security
    We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data from:
        Unauthorized access
        Data breaches
        Alteration or destruction of data

    However, no online platform can guarantee complete security of transmitted information.

9. Cookies and Tracking
    The platform may use cookies or similar technologies to:
        Maintain login sessions
        Improve user experience
        Analyze platform usage

    Users may disable cookies through their browser settings, although some features may not function properly.

10. Children’s Privacy
    The platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age required by law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

    Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Information
    For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the platform administrators through the official support or contact page.
